F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of AI ethics solutions do you offer?

We specialize in building responsible AI systems with built-in bias detection, explainability (XAI), compliance tracking, and privacy-preserving training methods. These are ideal for businesses prioritizing fairness, transparency, and governance.

What is Suraksha Setu and how does it help in disaster management?

Suraksha Setu is a disaster management platform that offers real-time updates, SOS alerts, and location-based rescue coordination. It's designed to empower citizens and responders during critical emergencies using tech-first solutions.

How does your Blood Bank Portal improve access to blood donations?

Our Blood Bank Portal connects donors and recipients efficiently through smart location-based matching, real-time availability, and a responsive alert system. It reduces delays and saves lives with a streamlined, user-friendly interface.

Is your healthcare AI platform (Vaidhya) reliable for rural or remote areas?

Yes, Vaidhya is designed to work with minimal connectivity and offers real-time recommendations using a lightweight AI model. It’s also scalable with voice input/output for accessibility.

What is the Solar Paint project and how does it work?

It’s a next-gen energy solution where solar-harvesting paint is coupled with our AIDE IoT device to monitor and optimize energy usage. Ideal for sustainable architecture and low-cost solar deployment.

How does Kisan Kart help farmers and buyers?

Kisan Kart connects local farmers to consumers directly via an easy-to-use MERN stack platform, removing middlemen and ensuring fair pricing, live stock updates, and payment integration.

Do you support custom project collaborations or tech consulting?

Yes, we work with startups, institutions, and organizations for customized AI, web, and IoT solutions. We also provide tech architecture consulting, ethical review, and system prototyping.
